Focus Coach

Nowadays it's so easy to get distracted in the work environment. This project is aimed to help people work without distraction.

To validate and answer this problem, I interviewed 6 people. The interviewees include 3 engineers, 1 PM and 2 designers. When asked about a successful side project, they all mentioned the importance of sharing the idea with the others.

How can we bridge the gap between having an idea, and sharing an idea with the others?

Low-fidelity mockups

After brainstorming the solutions, I decided to design a sharing social network for people to post ideas. I used InVision and put together a clickable mockup. and a flow to test it.

The feedback were mostly around whom are the ideas shared to. Some people are concerned about making the ideas too public. I modified the social network concept, so that the user can only share it with colleagues or friends they choose.

My early mockup also used the business canvas model to help people to think through when adding an idea. However, during the interview, I found it's too laborious to fill in the whole canvas. So I changed the input to something more flexible. Now the user can use multi-media to capture the problem they discovered or a thought they had.

Comment on ideas

With the "inspire" button, just as the Facebook like button, the contributors can be acknowledged when her comment inspires other people.

The app is a community that people can share and get feedback about their ideas, and the value of idea sharing, essentially, is to help people start to take actions beyond just generating ideas.

I demoed it during the Citrix Hack Week, and won the Best Standalong Product category in US West Region. I'm currently working with a developer to implement the design.
