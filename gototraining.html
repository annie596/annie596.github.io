GoToTraining Android

GoToTraining Android app allows training attendees to attend live training from anywhere. As the design lead, I redesigned the app from Feburary to June 2015.

What is a great online training experience for learners? When I asked myself when I first started the project.

Notes from the attendee interview
Information architecture

Analyzing 6 interviews with the online training attendees, I discovered 4 themes that will encourage learnig: asking questions, interacting with others, taking notes, and learning by doing. From site visits and interview with the trainer, engagement and interaction are also the most important factors in determine if the training is successful.

How can we encourage interaction during the training?

As the redesign project scope doesn't include functionality changes, I chose 2 existing function to focus on - chat and hand raise.

After trying out possible layouts and discussing with developers, I narrowed down the direction. All the secondary functions are hidden in a right side panel.

Layout exploration

I went further and started to think about how to make chat more prominent in the UI to encourage the uage.

The 2 floating button for chat and hand raise catch attention from the attendees. When in full screen, the buttons move to the corner to create an immersive viewing experience.

Combining the existing color palette and the material design guidelines, I created the visual theme for the app. During the visual exploartion, I spent some time on the iconography, and made sure that the icon works for color blind as well.

Designing for accessibility
The empty chat screen encourages the attendees to send chat messages.

Beside the navigation refresh, I also added small details here and there (for example, in the empty chat screen and onboarding) in the app to encourage chat and hand raise.

There's significant improvement in the usage of chat and hand raise, comparing to metrics before.

Of course, this is just our MVP to our redesign. There are many other improvements throughout the app that the team is still working on.
